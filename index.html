The Wizarding World of Giphy!

Where you can make images move by tapping them with your wand.

To conjure gifs, tap a button of our choice with your wand and say "Accio gif".

To add a new button, enter a spell into the spell box, tap the Aparecium button, and say "Aparecium button".

To make an image move, tap it with your wand.

To remove all images, press the Obsoletum Picturae button.
